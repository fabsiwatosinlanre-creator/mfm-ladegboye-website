About Us

Mountain of Fire and Miracle Ministries (MFM) Ladegboye Region is a prayer-driven church committed to raising champions through intense prayer, the undiluted Word of God, and the power of the Holy Spirit. Our mandate is to liberate destinies, restore hope, and transform lives through the ministry of prayer and holiness.
